FocusFlow AI - FAQ

General Questions

FocusFlow AI is a Chrome extension designed to help you track and improve your productivity by monitoring the time you spend on websites. It categorizes your browsing into Productive, Unproductive, and Neutral activities, provides a productivity score, and offers actionable tips to stay focused.

FocusFlow AI tracks the time you spend on websites and assigns them to Productive, Unproductive, or Neutral categories based on your settings. It calculates a productivity score, visualizes your time usage in a pie chart, and provides personalized tips to help you minimize distractions and focus on what matters.

Yes, FocusFlow AI is free to use. Simply install it from the Chrome Web Store, and start tracking your productivity right away.

Feature-Specific Questions

The productivity score is calculated as a percentage of your Productive time compared to your total tracked time (Productive + Unproductive + Neutral). For example, if you spend 60 minutes on Productive sites out of a total of 100 minutes, your score will be 60%.

- Productive: Time spent on websites you've categorized as productive (e.g., docs.google.com, work-related sites).
- Unproductive: Time spent on websites you've categorized as unproductive (e.g., facebook.com, social media).
- Neutral: Time spent on websites that aren't categorized as either productive or unproductive.

To make the stats easier to read, FocusFlow AI automatically converts times to the most appropriate unit. If the time is less than 60 minutes, it's shown in minutes (e.g., 45 min). If it's 60 minutes or more, it's shown in hours (e.g., 2 hr 30 min). If it's 1440 minutes (24 hours) or more, it's shown in days (e.g., 2 d).

The pie chart visually represents your time distribution across Productive, Unproductive, and Neutral categories for the selected period (Today, Yesterday, or All Time). Each segment's size reflects the proportion of time spent in that category.
